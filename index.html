About Me

I want to save the world. OK, maybe that's a bit much. But, I do want my time to be spent making things better.

While working in non-profits, I applied for an email marketing position that required basic HTML for an interview. Before the interview, I sat down on the couch to teach myself and time traveled 5 hours - I was hooked. I took up learning JavaScript and never looked back.

Since then, I have worked on many small projects to improve my skills and am excited to pursue a career in web development. Ultimately, I hope to integrate new technologies into nonprofits or other companies to help improve peoples' lives.
